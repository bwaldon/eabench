As a university, we determined through a disciplinary process that a 20-year-old student violated campus policy related to alcohol possession.The student has explicitly objected to any disclosure of this information to their parents and has not provided consent. The university is considering notifying the student’s parents about the violation under FERPA’s drug and alcohol provision. However, some administrators are concerned that doing so without the student’s consent could violate FERPA, particularly because the student is enrolled in a postsecondary institution and holds FERPA rights. We want to know whether we are permitted to disclose this information to the parents despite the student’s objection.
