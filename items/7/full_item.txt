Final output: Yes, FERPA permits you to comply with a federal grand jury subpoena. But there is an important caveat that distinguishes this from an ordinary subpoena: under a federal grand jury subpoena, the court is required to order your school not to disclose the existence or contents of the subpoena to anyone—including the student’s parents. This means the usual FERPA requirement that you notify parents in advance of compliance does not apply here; in fact, your federal funding is at risk if you notify the parents in advance.
