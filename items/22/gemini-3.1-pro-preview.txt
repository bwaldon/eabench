### The Legal Basis: Section (b)(1)(L)
Under FERPA subsection **(b)(1)**, no funds shall be made available to an educational agency that releases education records without parental consent *except* to specific parties. 

Subsection **(b)(1)(L)** explicitly permits disclosure without parental consent to:
> *"an agency caseworker or other representative of a State or local child welfare agency... who has the right to access a student's case plan... when such agency or organization is legally responsible... for the care and protection of the student..."*

Because the student is in foster care and the county child welfare agency is legally responsible for their care, the caseworker qualifies under this exception. Do not forget your record-keeping duties. Under subsection **(b)(4)(A)**, your school must maintain a log kept with the student's education records.
